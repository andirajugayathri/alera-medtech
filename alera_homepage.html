Our Approach

We set out to change this.

While other areas of patient care have made tremendous strides, these essential devices have not kept pace.

See our approach

In neurology and vascular access, many devices used routinely — hypodermic needle electrodes, IONM electrodes, catheter securement devices, prefilled syringes, infection control caps — have changed very little over the past several decades.

We apply modern engineering, advanced materials, and thoughtful clinician-centered design to reimagine these products entirely.

By avoiding traditional development delays, we bring advancements to market more quickly — leading to fewer complications, reduced system costs, and stronger outcomes for patients.
