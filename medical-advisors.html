Medical Advisors

BARBARA ANN GRECO, M.D.

MEDICAL ADVISOR

Dr. Greco was Primary Transplant Nephrologist/Nephrology Director, Baystate Transplant Program, UMass Chan Baystate Medical Center, Springfield, MA. She practiced nephrology medicine for more than 35 years and was Associate Professor of Medicine, UMass Chan School of Medicine and was an Assistant Professor of Medicine at Vanderbilt University Medical Center, Nashville, TN. A graduate of Harvard Medical School, she did her internal medicine training at Mass General Hospital and nephrology training at Vanderbilt.

Dr. Greco’s clinical research and studies include over 40 publications, books and chapters, as well as active roles in dozens of clinical studies.

SHAI N.GOZANI, M.D. , PH.D.

MEDICAL ADVISOR

Dr. Gozani, M.D., Ph.D., is the Founder/CEO of NeuroMetrix, a spin-off from MIT. He has published over 30 peer-reviewed scientific articles and has been awarded 25 U.S. and international patents.

Dr. Gozani holds a B.A. degree in Computer Science, a M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science (Biomedical Engineering focus) and a Ph.D. in Neurobiology, from the University of California, Berkeley. He also received his M.D. from Harvard Medical School and the Harvard-MIT Division of Health Sciences and Technology at M.I.T.

MAHESH DESAI, M.D.

MEDICAL ADVISOR

Dr. Desai is the Head of Robotic Surgery at the Centre for Robotic Surgery (CRS), and co-founder and Managing Trustee at Muljibhai Patel Urological Hospital (MPUH) in Nadiad, India. One of the most renowned urologists in India, he has published over 750 scientific papers in the fields of urology, prostate cancer and robotic surgery.

Dr. Desai has served as President of the World Endourological Society, the Société Internationale d'Urologie and the Urological Society of India.

DAVID R. STASKIN, M.D.

MEDICAL ADVISOR

Dr. David Staskin, MD is a Urology Specialist in Brighton, MA and has over 44 years of experience in the medical field after graduating from Hahnemann Medical College and Hospital in 1979. He is affiliated with St. Elizabeth's Medical Center and serves on the faculty of Tufts University since 2008.

Dr. Staskin has published extensively (> 150 articles) in the areas of female urology, male voiding dysfunction, BPH and LUTS, neuro-urology, and urodynamics. He is an editor of several textbooks, is the inventor of the SPARC Sling System and has contributed significantly to the development of the Monarc, BioArc, and RetroArc sling systems.

PAUL A. CHURCH, M.D.

MEDICAL ADVISOR

Dr. Paul Church was a practicing Urologist for more than 35 years and Assistant Professor of Surgery at Harvard Medical School in Boston, Massachusetts before retirement in 2017. He was also involved with clinical research, including studies on the early detection of prostate cancer utilizing digital rectal exam, transrectal ultrasound, and PSA.

Dr. Church served as an investigator for “Prostaglove,” precursor to the current ProstaMetrix device. He brings deep clinical experience diagnosing and treating prostatic disorders and background in industry-sponsored clinical trials.
