Cernode Electro-diagnostic Products

Critical Care

Securement

SureSet Securement Devices

Vascular access devices (VADs) play a critical role in modern patient care. Despite their importance, these devices are frequently at risk for dislodgement, migration, and infection.

SureSet™ SafeTrak™ Strain Relief Technology represents an innovative approach by focusing on the entire catheter pathway. Unlike conventional methods, SafeTrak™ specifically targets and minimizes mechanical stress at the insertion site.

Protects against accidental dislodgement and migration, contributing to improved patient safety and greater comfort.

Visit suresetsecure.com
Flush Syringe

Duoflush™ Integrated Prefilled Flush Syringes

Duoflush is an innovative prefilled saline flush syringe with an integrated disinfection unit. It targets essential IV-line maintenance in neurology and critical care settings.

  • Integrated disinfection cap with 70% isopropyl alcohol pad.
  • Prefilled 10 mL saline syringe (0.9% sodium chloride).
  • Ergonomic design with textured grip and one-handed activation.
  • Universal Luer-lock compatibility for seamless integration.

Studies show Duoflush can nearly double compliance rates compared to separate alcohol swabs and syringes.
